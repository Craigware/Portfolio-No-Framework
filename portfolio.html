I am an artist turned web developer. I started taking coding more seriously when I was in college because I wanted to make my artwork interactive. Front-end development and game development has been my favorite outlets for such. Through my experience learning I have come to find out I very much enjoy all aspects of software development. The creativity and problem solving of front-end is very engaging for me while the organization and management of data has proven to be quite satisfying in the back-end.

I am currently looking for an entry or junior level position in either front-end development, back-end development or full-stack development. My favorite frameworks to use have been React for front-end, and FastAPI or Django for back-end. Although I do love learning different technologies, I currently have specific interest in learning ASP.NET due to C# being my favorite programming language.
